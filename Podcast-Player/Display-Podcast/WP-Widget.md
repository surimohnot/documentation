While WordPress is transitioning to editor blocks, you can still use traditional widgets by installing the **Classic Widgets** plugin. This allows you to place your Podcast Player in various widget-ready areas like sidebars, footers, or headers. Here's how you can add the Podcast Player widget to your WordPress website. ### Step-by-Step Guide to Adding a Podcast Player Widget

#### Step 1: Install the Classic Widgets Plugin

 Since WordPress is phasing out native widgets in favor of editor blocks, you'll need to install the **Classic Widgets** plugin to continue using the older widget functionality.
1. Go to **Plugins > Add New** in your WordPress dashboard.
2. Search for **Classic Widgets** in the search bar.
3. Install and activate the plugin.

 This will restore the old widget system, allowing you to add classic widgets like the Podcast Player.

#### Step 2: Access the Widgets Page

1. In your WordPress dashboard, navigate to **Appearance > Widgets**.
2. This will take you to the Widgets page where you can manage and place widgets in your site's widget-ready areas.

#### Step 3: Choose a Widget Area

1. On the **Widgets** page, you'll see a list of widget-ready areas on the right side. These areas are defined by your theme and could include:
  - Sidebars
  - Footers
  - Headers
  - Other custom widget areas
2. Choose the area where you want to add the podcast player.

#### Step 4: Add the Podcast Player Widget

1. In the available widgets list on the left, look for the **Podcast Player** widget.
2. **Drag and drop** the Podcast Player widget into your desired widget area.

#### Step 5: Configure the Podcast Player Widget

 Once you've placed the widget, you can configure its settings:
1. **Enter Your Podcast Feed URL**: In the widget settings, you'll find a field to enter your **Podcast Feed URL**.
2. **Customize the Player**: Depending on the podcast player plugin you're using, additional customization options may be available, such as:
  - Display style options
  - Show/hide player elements
  - Changing colors to match your branding

 These settings are generally easy to adjust and can be found within the widget configuration panel.

#### Step 6: Save Your Changes

1. After configuring the podcast player, make sure to **click the "Save" button** to apply your changes.
2. Your Podcast Player will now be live and visible on your website in the widget area you selected.
