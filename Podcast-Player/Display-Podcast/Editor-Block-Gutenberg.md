WordPress makes it simple to embed a podcast player on your site through its block editor, without requiring any coding or shortcode. Follow these steps to add and customize a podcast player block in a post or page: [easy_tubecasting instance="1" url="https://youtu.be/94eK3BWtWtA?si=qWBIcyzXD3c019jV"]

### Step 1: Open the WordPress Editor

1. Navigate to your WordPress dashboard.
2. To create a new post, go to **Posts > Add New**or Edit an existing post.
3. To create a new page, go to **Pages > Add New**or Edit an existing page.

 This will launch the WordPress block editor where you can add content and blocks.

### Step 2: Add a Podcast Player Block

1. In the editor, click the **“+”** button in the top-left corner or within the editor interface.
2. From the block selection menu, type **"Podcast Player"** in the search bar or scroll through the available block options.
3. Select the **Podcast Player** block to insert it into your post or page.

### Step 3: Enter Your Podcast Feed URL

 Once the block is added to your editor, you’ll be prompted to enter your podcast’s feed URL: In the provided text field, input the **podcast feed URL** from your podcast host platform. Once the URL is entered, the player will automatically load your podcast episodes and display them in the block.

### Step 4: Customize the Podcast Player Block

 [easy_tubecasting instance="1" url="https://youtu.be/bUBTlNMDT7E?si=1uKs3wFFKZNzBopl"] The WordPress block editor allows you to easily customize the podcast player directly from the right-hand sidebar. The customization options include: **Display Layouts**: Select from different layout styles to match your website’s design. **Show or Hide Elements**: Toggle on/off elements like the search bar, episode descriptions, or episode numbers to simplify the player interface. **Add Subscription Buttons**: Add subscription buttons for popular platforms like Apple Podcasts, Spotify, or Google Podcasts to allow your visitors to subscribe directly from the player. **Sort Episodes**: Sort podcast episodes either by **date** or **episode title** depending on how you want the episodes to appear. **Change Accent Color**: You can adjust the player’s accent color to match your website’s color scheme for a consistent brand experience. Feel free to explore and modify these settings to achieve the desired look and functionality.

### Step 5: Publish or Update Your Post/Page

 After customizing the player block, you’re ready to publish the content:
1. Review the appearance of the podcast player.
2. Once satisfied, click **Publish** (for new content) or **Update** (for existing content) to make the changes live.
