# Shortcode Generator

Shortcodes have always been a flexible and reliable way to add your podcast player to posts, pages, templates, and even page builders. Many of our users rely on them because they just work. However, customizing a shortcode manually can be frustrating. There is no live preview and it can be hard to remember all the available attributes.

That’s why we built the Shortcode Generator. It allows you to create and customize your podcast player visually while seeing real-time updates. Once you're happy with the look and settings, the shortcode is automatically generated for you.

Here’s how to use it:

 [easy_tubecasting instance="1" url="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_PghSa5GAQI"]
1. Go to your WordPress dashboard
2. Click on **Podcast Player > Shortcode**
3. Select **Create New Shortcode**
4. Choose an existing podcast or enter a new podcast feed URL
5. Customize the player using the available options
6. Click **Generate Shortcode** and then **Copy Shortcode**
7. Paste the shortcode anywhere you want to display the player — in the Block Editor, Classic Editor, or any page builder

That’s it. No guesswork. No complex setup. Just a faster and more intuitive way to get your podcast on your site.
