Global settings affect Podcast Player behavior across the site. Pro adds sticky, cookie, statistics, transcript, and play-count options.

## Steps

1. Go to **Podcast Player > Settings**.
2. Review the **General**, **Design**, **Optimization & Security**, and **Advanced** sections.
3. Change the settings needed for the site.
4. Save the settings.
5. Clear caches if player scripts or styles are cached.

## Pro Settings

- **Keep Player Visible While Scrolling**: Turns supported default or legacy players into a bottom sticky player when the active player scrolls out of view.
- **Show Last Played Episode in Bottom Sticky Player**: Uses a cookie to restore the currently playing episode on revisit or page navigation.
- **Collect Podcast Player Stats (Beta)**: Tracks basic play and search statistics.
- **Show Episode Transcripts (Beta)**: Displays transcripts when the podcast feed provides supported transcript/caption data.
- **Episode play statistics threshold**: Number of seconds playback must pass before a play is counted.

## Related Core Settings

- **Podcast update interval**: How often stored feeds are checked for updates.
- **Image Optimization**: Saves podcast images to the Media Library for responsive delivery.
- **Protect Podcast Data from Exposure**: Masks sensitive feed/audio data in front-end source where possible.
- **Episode date timezone**: Chooses GMT/UTC, site local timezone, or feed timezone.
- **Enable AJAX page compatibility**: Helps themes that navigate without full page reloads.
