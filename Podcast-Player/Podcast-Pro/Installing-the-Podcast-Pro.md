Podcast Player Pro extends the free Podcast Player plugin. The Pro plugin will not load its Pro features unless the free plugin is installed, active, and meets the required version.

## Requirements

- Podcast Player free version installed and activated.
- Podcast Player free version `8.2.0` or higher.
- Podcast Player Pro plugin ZIP or installed plugin folder.
- PHP `5.6` or higher.

 Follow these steps to install the Pro version of the Podcast Player: [easy_tubecasting instance="1" url="https://youtu.be/Bwfyrwwo3UU?si=fzzAYzAoLz24pJbO"]

### Step 1: Download the Pro Version

 After purchasing the Pro version, you will be redirected to a page where you can **download the latest version** of the plugin as a **zip file**. Additionally, a **download link** will be sent to your registered email address.
- **Can’t find the download link?** No worries. Simply log in to your account on the Podcast Player website and download the latest zip file from your account dashboard.

---

### Step 2: Upload the Plugin to WordPress

 Now that you have the Pro version zip file, you can upload it to your WordPress website:
1. In your **WordPress dashboard**, navigate to **Plugins** > **Add New**.
2. At the top of the page, click the **Upload Plugin** button.
3. Browse for the **zip file** you downloaded and upload it to your site.
4. Click **Install Now** to begin the installation process.

---

### Step 3: Activate the Plugin

 Once the plugin has been uploaded, you will see an option to **Activate** the plugin.
1. Click the **Activate** button to enable the Pro version of the Podcast Player.
2. Once activated, the Pro version will be listed among your installed plugins.

---

### Step 4: Start Using the Pro Version

 That’s it! You’re now ready to start using the Pro version of Podcast Player.
- **Access to Premium Features**: You will now have access to all the additional features and benefits of the Pro version, including multiple display layouts, a bottom sticky player, advanced episode import options, and much more.

---

### Conclusion

 By following these simple steps, you can install and activate the Pro version of Podcast Player on your WordPress website. With the Pro version, you unlock enhanced functionality that will help elevate your podcast presentation and improve user engagement. **Congratulations**, you're now set up to make the most of the advanced features of the Podcast Player Pro!
