The Sticky Player tool adds one bottom player across the site.

## Steps

1. Go to **Podcast Player > Toolkit > Sticky Player**.
2. Choose the audio source:

- **From Feed**
- **From Posts**
- **From Single Audio URL**

1. Complete the source fields.
2. Choose sticky player background and accent colors.
3. Choose whether to hide social sharing links.
4. Choose whether to hide the download link.
5. Click **Save Sticky Player**.
6. Visit the front end and confirm the sticky player appears when expected.

## Source Notes

- **From Feed**: Select a saved podcast or enter a feed URL.
- **From Posts**: Choose post type, taxonomy, and terms.
- **From Single Audio URL**: Enter the audio URL and optional title.

## Remove the Sticky Player

1. Go to **Toolkit > Sticky Player**.
2. Click **Remove Sticky Player**.

## Notes

- The saved sticky player uses a compact Pro list style internally.
- The global setting **Keep Player Visible While Scrolling** is separate. It makes an active normal player become sticky when it scrolls out of view.
