In this tutorial, we will go through how to use the **Podcast Player Statistics** feature. This feature will allow you to track how often episodes are played and the search terms used by your audience. Follow these steps to enable and view podcast statistics. [embed]https://youtu.be/gAVRozP8uqs?si=8kBwVQuyGzGhhmXL[/embed]

#### Step 1: Navigate to the Podcast Player Admin Page

- Go to your **WordPress dashboard**.
- In the sidebar, click on **Podcast Player**. You will see a list of your existing podcasts on the **home** section.

#### Step 2: Enable the Statistics Feature

- Click on the **Settings** tab in the Podcast Player admin.
- Find the option to **enable player statistics**.
- Once the setting is enabled, you can change the **play threshold time**. This is the minimum time an episode must be played to count in the statistics.

#### Step 3: Set Play Threshold Time

- Change the play threshold time to **2 seconds**. This means an episode needs to be played for at least 2 seconds to be counted in the statistics.

#### Step 4: View Statistics

- After enabling the statistics feature, return to the **home** section.
- You will now see a new **Stats** button for each podcast.
- Click the **Stats** button to view basic statistics. Since you just enabled the feature, there may not be any stats yet.

#### Step 5: Play Episodes and Check Statistics

- Play a few podcast episodes using the Podcast Player.
- Make sure the episode is played for at least **2 seconds** for it to count.
- After playing, **refresh the admin page** a few times to update the statistics.

#### Step 6: Analyze Statistics

- After a few weeks, your statistics page will start to show more data.
- You can view statistics for **daily, weekly, or monthly** periods by choosing the appropriate option in the **chart** section.

#### Step 7: Episode-Level Statistics

- The statistics feature also offers detailed information for individual episodes.
- Click on any episode to view **episode-specific** play statistics.

#### Step 8: View Search Terms

- The podcast player will track all **search terms** entered by users in the player.
- To test, enter a search term in the Podcast Player, and then check the stats page. You will see the search term appear in the statistics.

#### Conclusion

 The statistics feature is a valuable tool to help you understand which episodes are popular and what your audience is searching for.
