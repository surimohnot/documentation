The Bulk Audio tool adds single audio players across posts where an audio URL already exists.

## Supported Audio Sources

- **Blubrry PowerPress** enclosure data.
- **Seriously Simple Podcasting** `audio_file` metadata.
- **Custom Field** containing an audio URL.

## Steps

1. Go to **Podcast Player > Toolkit > Bulk Audio**.
2. Choose where each post should get its audio URL.
3. If using **Custom Field**, enter the custom field name.
4. Choose where the player should appear:

- After post content.
- Before post content.

1. Choose player display style.
2. Optionally choose a subscription menu and primary link count.
3. Set background and accent colors.
4. Choose whether to hide social links, download link, titles, or featured images.
5. Click **Add Bulk Players**.

## Remove Bulk Players

1. Go to **Toolkit > Bulk Audio**.
2. Click **Remove Bulk Players**.

## Notes

- The tool saves a global bulk player configuration.
- It does not create new posts.
- Posts without a valid audio URL from the selected source are skipped.
