Creating a single episode podcast player using just an audio URL is simple with Podcast Player Pro. Follow these steps to easily embed a single episode player into your WordPress site: [embed]https://youtu.be/VOAuVq_NE8s?si=bDoFrv3-o4peLRw9[/embed]

### Step 1: Add a Podcast Player Block

- Open the page or post where you want to embed the podcast episode.
- **Create a new Podcast Player block**.

### Step 2: Select the "From Audio/Video URL" Option

- On the right-hand sidebar, you will find the **Fetch Podcasts** dropdown.
- Select **"From Audio/Video URL"**.

### Step 3: Enter the Audio URL

- In the text field that appears, **enter the URL of your audio file** (MP3 or other supported formats).

### Step 4: Customize the Episode

- **Add a title** for the episode.
- You can also configure any other optional settings (such as show notes, artwork, or playback options).

### Step 5: Preview and Publish

- **Preview** the player to ensure everything is set up as expected.
- Once you're satisfied, click **Publish** or **Update** the post.

### Using a Shortcode for Single Episode Player

 You can also display a single episode player using a shortcode. Here’s how:
1. **Add a shortcode block** to the post or page.
2. Enter the following shortcode structure:

```
[[podcastplayer fetch_method="link" mediasrc="url_of_media_file"]]
```

### Step 6: Publish the Post

- Once everything looks good, **Publish** or **Update** the post.

### Conclusion:

 Your single episode podcast player is now live and ready for your listeners to enjoy. Whether using the block method or shortcode, the Podcast Player Pro gives you flexibility in how you display individual episodes using audio URLs. By following these steps, you'll easily embed a sleek and functional player for single podcast episodes on your WordPress site.
