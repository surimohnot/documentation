Selfhost Podcasting integrates seamlessly with the **Podcast Player** plugin (available from WordPress.org). When both plugins are active on your site, your podcast episodes become richer, with built-in playback capabilities directly on each episode’s WordPress post.

## How It Works

1. Install and activate the **Podcast Player** plugin on your WordPress site.
2. When you **create a new podcast episode** via Selfhost Podcasting, the plugin auto-injects a podcast player into the custom post that corresponds to the episode.
3. You can customize how this happens through the **Integrations** section of your Selfhost Podcasting settings:
  - Enable or disable automatic player insertion
  - Choose whether to insert the player as a **shortcode** or a **block**
  - Set a **default style** for the player

With this setup, each imported or new episode post becomes a **beautiful, fully functional podcast player post**, ready for listeners to play directly on your site.

## What the Podcast Player Offers

When integrating with Selfhost Podcasting, here are the key features of the free Podcast Player plugin that your users will benefit from:

- **Responsive design**
  The player adapts to screen size and container width, making it look good on desktop, tablet, or mobile devices.
- **Playback controls and enhancements**
  The free version includes play/pause, next/previous episode navigation, speed control, jump forward/skip back, and social sharing buttons.
- **Customizable visibility of elements**
  You have control over whether to show or hide things like the episode title, description, or artwork.
- **Support for multiple player instances**
  You can place more than one podcast player (for different episodes or shows) on the same page or post.

These features mean your listeners get a polished listening experience, while you maintain full control over branding and content.

## Benefits of This Integration

- **No extra work for users**
  When creating an episode, the player shows up automatically.
- **Consistent branded experience**
  All episodes will have your chosen style and player layout.
- **Better listener engagement**
  Because the episodes are playable right on your site, users are more likely to stick around and listen.
- **Flexible content control**
  Since the podcast feed description and your WordPress post content stay distinct, you can add richer content without affecting the feed.
