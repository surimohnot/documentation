Once your podcast details are complete, it’s time to add episodes.

1. Click on the **Episodes** tab.
2. You’ll see two options:
  - **Create New Episode** – add a new episode manually.
  - **Import Episodes** – bring in existing episodes from another podcast feed.

### Creating a New Episode Manually

When creating a new episode, you’ll again find three sections: **Required**, **Recommended**, and **Advanced**.

#### Required Information

You must provide:

- The **audio file URL** (or upload it to your WordPress media folder)
- The **episode title**
- The **episode description**

Once these fields are filled, your podcast feed with both podcast-level and episode-level details is ready for distribution.

You can now submit your feed to **Apple Podcasts**, **Spotify**, or any other podcasting app, or embed it in a podcast player on your site.
