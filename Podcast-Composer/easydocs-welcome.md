**Podcast Composer** is a powerful WordPress plugin designed for podcasters, networks, and content curators who need granular control over their RSS feeds. Unlike standard podcasting plugins that simply host a single show, Podcast Composer acts as a **distribution engine**.

It allows you to take episodes from existing "Source" feeds and reorganize them into entirely new "Output" feeds.

### Why Use Podcast Composer?

- **Network Consolidation**: Combine multiple individual show feeds into one "Network Master Feed."
- **Niche Spin-offs**: Create a new feed containing only specific episodes (e.g., "Best of" or "Interviews Only") from your main show.
- **Content Reruns**: Schedule and distribute older episodes into a fresh feed for new listeners.
- **Feed Customization**: Modify titles, descriptions, and artwork for episodes in your new feed without changing the original source content.

### How It Works

The plugin uses a simple three-step architecture:

1. **Projects**: You create project workspace to group sources and outputs together. 
2. **Sources**: You connect existing podcast RSS feeds as content sources.
3. **Outputs**: You map specific episodes from your sources into a new, standards-compliant RSS feed generated by your WordPress site.
