Welcome to Podcast Composer! This guide will walk you through creating your first custom feed in minutes.

### 1. Create a Project

Everything in the plugin happens inside a **Project**. A project is a workspace where you manage a specific group of sources and outputs.

1. Click on **Podcast Composer** in your sidebar.
2. Click **Create New Project**.
3. Give your project a name (e.g., "My Network") and click **Save**.

![Add a new project workspace](https://easypodcastpro.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/project-860x329.png)

### 2. Add a Source Feed

A "Source" is any existing podcast RSS feed you want to pull episodes from.

1. Inside your Project, go to the **Sources** tab.
2. Click **Add Source**.
3. Paste the URL of the podcast RSS feed you want to use.
4. Click **Sync**. The plugin will now fetch all episodes from that feed and index them in your site.

![Add source podcast feed](https://easypodcastpro.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Add-Sources.png)

### 3. Create an Output Feed

An "Output" is the new feed you are creating for distribution.

1. Go to the **Outputs** tab in your project.
2. Click **Create Output Feed**.
3. Set a **Title** for your new podcast. This will be part of your new feed's URL, e.g., `yoursite.com/feed/my-new-show`.

![](https://easypodcastpro.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Create-Output.png)

### 4. Add Podcast Meta to the output feed

1. Go to the **Podcast Metadata** tab of your newly create output podcast.
2. Add podcast metadata details like **Title**, **Description**, and **Artwork**etc.

![Add output podcast metadata details](https://easypodcastpro.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/podcast-meta.png)

### 5. Map Your Episodes

Now for the fun part: choosing which episodes go into your new feed.

1. Go to the **Episodes** or **Library** view in your project.
2. You will see a list of all episodes fetched from your sources.
3. Use the **Add to Output** button (or drag and drop if available) to move episodes into your desired Output feed.
4. Rearrange the order if necessary.

![Assign source episodes to output podcast](https://easypodcastpro.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/add-episodes-1024x533.png)

### 6. Share Your New Feed

1. Go back to the **Outputs** tab.
2. Copy the **Feed URL**.
3. You can now submit this URL to Apple Podcasts, Spotify, or any other directory!
